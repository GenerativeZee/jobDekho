# 01 — Vision & Overview

## Product
A centralized, AI-powered job discovery platform for India. Starting niche: **Government Jobs only (Phase 1)**, then expanding to Freshers + Private Jobs (Phase 2), and eventually a complete employment ecosystem.

## Problem
Job seekers must visit SSC, UPSC, Railways, Banking websites, State PSC sites, company career portals, and job boards separately. The market is completely fragmented. Most unemployed people — especially in Tier 2/3 cities — miss opportunities because they never see them.

Today, jobs are scattered across:
- Government portals
- State government recruitment websites
- PSU careers pages
- Private company career pages
- Startup hiring pages
- Job boards
- Walk-in hiring notifications
- Apprenticeship programs
- Contract jobs
- Freelance opportunities

## Solution
One platform where users:
- Create a profile once (education, skills, location, job type preference)
- Get a personalized feed of all relevant openings
- Receive instant alerts via Telegram / WhatsApp / Email
- Apply via redirect to the official source (avoids legal issues)

## Vision
Become the **"Google for Jobs in India"** — especially for Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

---

## Target Users
- Tier 2 / Tier 3 city job seekers
- Students and freshers (18–28 age group)
- Government exam aspirants
- Unemployed or underemployed adults with limited digital literacy

---

## Market Size
- India: 900M+ working-age population
- ~40M actively searching for jobs at any point
- Government exam aspirants alone: 20M+/year
- UPSC, SSC, Railway, Banking draw tens of millions of applicants annually

---

## Competitor Weaknesses

| Platform | Weakness |
|---|---|
| Naukri, LinkedIn | No government jobs, Tier 1 focused |
| Sarkari Result | Poor UX, no personalization, ad-heavy |
| Employment News | Static, no alerts |
| FreshersWorld | Outdated UI, poor trust signals |
| State portals | Siloed, hard to navigate |

---

## Opportunity Gap
No existing platform combines:
- Government + Private + Internships in one feed
- AI-based matching
- WhatsApp/Telegram-first alerts
- Vernacular language support
- Mobile-first UX for low-bandwidth users

---

## Niche Decision: Government Jobs First

### Option A: Government Jobs Only
**Pros:**
- Clear audience, high intent users
- Deadlines create urgency and repeat traffic
- Strong SEO opportunity
- Exam prep = premium revenue stream

**Cons:**
- Slower job volume growth
- Revenue depends on premium subscriptions

### Option B: Freshers Jobs Only
**Pros:**
- Larger immediate audience
- Private companies easier to partner with
- Faster content velocity

**Cons:**
- High competition from Naukri, Internshala
- Lower user retention

### Decision: Start with Government Jobs
Build trust in the most underserved, high-intent segment first. Expand to freshers/private in Phase 2.

**Core Hypothesis:** A focused portal with 10,000 high-quality jobs and excellent alerts will outperform a portal with 1 million poorly categorized listings.
