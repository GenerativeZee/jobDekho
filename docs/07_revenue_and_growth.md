# 07 — Revenue Model & Growth Strategy

---

## Revenue Model

### Phased Approach

**Phase 1 (Months 1–6): No Revenue**
Focus entirely on users, data quality, and alerts. Build trust first. Don't monetize too early.

**Phase 2 (Months 6–12): First Revenue**
Launch Premium subscription only. Keep it simple.

**Phase 3 (Year 2): Full Revenue Stack**
All 5 revenue streams active.

---

### Revenue Stream 1: Premium Subscription

**Pricing:** Rs.49–199/month

| Feature | Free | Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Browse all jobs | Yes | Yes |
| Save jobs | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| Email digest | Weekly | Daily |
| Telegram alerts | Yes | Instant + Priority |
| AI match score | No | Yes |
| Deadline reminders | No | Yes |
| WhatsApp alerts | No | Yes |
| Resume analysis | No | 1 free/month |

### Revenue Stream 2: Featured Job Listings

Companies and recruiting firms pay to feature their listings prominently in the feed.

**Pricing:** Rs.500–5,000 per featured listing (depending on duration and placement)

**Display:** "Featured" badge on job card, shown at top of relevant category feeds.

Aspirants spend heavily on coaching. This can become a major revenue stream.

| Product | Price |
|---|---|

| Full Exam Prep (monthly) | Rs.499–999/month |

---

### Revenue Stream 5: Recruitment SaaS for SMEs

Allow small and medium businesses to:
- Post jobs directly
- Track applicants through a dashboard
- Schedule interviews
- Send bulk communications

**Pricing:** Rs.999–4,999/month (tiered by job posts and candidates)

---

### Revenue Stream 6: Affiliate Commissions

Partner with:
- Online coaching institutes (Unacademy, Testbook, Adda247)
- Skill development platforms
- Online course platforms (Coursera, Udemy)

**Model:** CPA (cost per acquisition) — earn commission when user purchases via referral link.

## Growth Strategy

**Core principle:** Technology alone will not win. Distribution, trust, and user experience determine success.

---

### Channel 1: Telegram (Primary Growth Engine)
- Create a public channel from Day 1 — before the website even launches
- Auto-post every new government job with deadline and direct link
- Target: 100,000 subscribers by Month 12
- Cost: Rs.0 (purely organic)

---

### Channel 2: WhatsApp Community
- Create a WhatsApp community for job alerts
- Manual curation initially; automate via API in Phase 2
- Tier 2/3 users are heavily on WhatsApp

---

### Channel 3: YouTube Shorts
- Daily video: "Top 10 Government Jobs Today"
- Use AI-generated or template-based short videos
- Drives brand awareness, SEO backlinks, and subscriber trust
- Repurpose Telegram content into short videos

---

### Channel 4: SEO (Most Scalable Long-Term)

Auto-generate landing pages for every high-volume keyword:

```
Government Jobs 2026
Latest Railway Jobs
SSC Jobs 2026
Jobs for 12th Pass
Banking Jobs for Graduates
Government Jobs in Delhi
Government Jobs in UP
Government Jobs in Bihar
Government Jobs in Maharashtra
State-wise job pages (all 28 states + UTs)
Qualification-wise pages
Category-wise pages
```

**Why this works:** Government job seekers heavily use Google. These pages compound over time and deliver free, high-intent traffic indefinitely.

---

### Channel 5: Referral Program (Phase 2)
- "Refer a friend → both get 1 month Premium free"
- Drives viral word-of-mouth
- Especially powerful in Tier 2/3 cities where jobs are shared person-to-person

---

### Channel 6: Content Marketing
- Blog posts: "How to prepare for SBI PO 2026", "SSC CGL syllabus guide"
- These rank on Google and build authority
- Drives both organic traffic and trust
